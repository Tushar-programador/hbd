# Project Vision

This project is not a birthday website.

It is a digital love letter.

A story told through photographs, memories, music, animations, and heartfelt words.

Every page should make her feel like she is walking through the beautiful moments you've shared together.

The objective isn't to impress with technology.

The objective is to remind her how loved she is.

When she reaches the final page, she should smile, laugh at old memories, and maybe even shed a few happy tears.

---

# Primary Goal

Build a premium, interactive birthday experience that feels personal, emotional, elegant, and unforgettable.

The website should celebrate:

* Her birthday
* Your relationship
* Your memories
* Your future together

# Success Criteria

The project is successful if she:

* Smiles throughout the journey.
* Feels genuinely loved and appreciated.
* Relives your happiest memories together.
* Discovers thoughtful surprises.
* Wants to revisit the website long after her birthday.

The true measure of success is not code quality or animation smoothness—it's creating a gift that feels deeply personal, thoughtful, and unforgettable. Every transition, photo, and message should reflect the care and love that went into building it.
